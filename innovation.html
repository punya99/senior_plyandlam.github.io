How Senior Plywoods creates
a difference

We know that when you work with us you can trust the quality of our product and service. That's why we only use the top quality materials in every project, so you can enjoy them for years to come. We only use premium grade plywood that's been treated with a special high-pressure process called 'green pressure impregnating'. This process preserves the natural beauty of our products, ensuring they'll stand out in any room. The biggest benefit of our high-quality plywood is that it holds up well against water and even fire damage. This means you'll never have to worry about your new home being damaged by natural disasters. Our premium plywood is made out of 100% recycled wood - we don't use any new lumber. This means our product is environmentally friendly and helps reduce the negative impact on our forests and aquatic habitats.

Innovative design is at the heart of our business. Senior Plywood listens and understands your needs, providing you with the unique solutions that you need for your home. Our senior plywood is designed to provide you with the perfect solution for your renovation project, while at the same time delivering exceptional value for money. We are never complacent and always innovating, so we're always on hand to help you out with any questions you may have about any of our products, or indeed any other questions about your renovation project. Senior Plywood is a premier supplier of plywood to the construction industry, providing quality products at competitive prices. Our commitment to customer satisfaction means that we are continually innovating our product line to meet the changing needs of our customers.
