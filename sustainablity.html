Environment

Our company is committed to using eco-friendly materials in our product production and since our inception, we've focused on making sure all of our products are safe for the environment. We use agro forestry timber that doesn't harm the environment and through a noninvasive approach for acquiring raw materials where nature is not disturbed.

We promise to use all the timber we procure from sustainable forests only. Even though we have an extensive variety of timbers and wood species, we have come up with a low-impact and long-lasting solution that is environmentally friendly. The secret to our success is not just the quality of our products, but also innovation. We use a combination of proven design and engineering, strong yet eco-friendly building materials coupled with natural resource management practices to achieve sustainable homes.
